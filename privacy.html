🔒 TorunHero Keyboard privacy first

📅 Last updated: 2026 — your data stays on your device

📘 Our commitment

TorunHero Keyboard respects your privacy and is built to protect your personal information. This policy explains exactly how your data is handled — no hidden tricks.

🚫 Information we do NOT collect

  • 🔐 Passwords & credit card numbers
  • 💬 Personal messages / chat content
  • 📇 Contact lists or address book
  • 📸 Photos, files or documents

✅ All typing data remains strictly on your device — it is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

⌨️ Keyboard input data

TorunHero Keyboard lets you type in Bangla and English. Every keystroke stays local:

  • ✓ Processed locally — only on your phone/tablet
  • ✓ We do not store, log, or monitor what you type — including passwords, sensitive texts or messages.
  • ✓ No cloud typing, no hidden analytics.

⚙️ Permissions we use

🌐 Internet
Used only for:
• displaying in-app updates
• optional online features (if any)
• future improvements
🔍 This permission is never used to collect personal data or keystrokes.

No other permissions (like contacts, storage, or location) are required for the keyboard to function.

🔗 Third‑party services

We may use trusted services to operate and improve the app:

  • Google Play Services — for distribution and basic platform features. They may collect limited, anonymous information as described in Google's privacy policy.

We don't control those third parties, but we only choose partners with strong privacy commitments.

🧸 Children’s privacy

TorunHero Keyboard is safe for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children — in fact, we collect nothing at all. If you are a parent and have concerns, feel free to contact us.

🛡️ Data security

Because TorunHero Keyboard does not store or transmit typing data, your private information stays on your device. We follow reasonable security practices to keep the app itself secure.

📌 Changes to this policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we’ll post the changes on this page. The “last updated” date at the top will always reflect the latest version.
