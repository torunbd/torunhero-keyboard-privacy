Privacy Policy – TorunHero Keyboard

Last updated: 2026

TorunHero Keyboard respects your privacy and is committed to protecting user information. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles data.

Information We Collect

TorunHero Keyboard does NOT collect or store personal information such as:

  • Passwords
  • Credit card numbers
  • Personal messages
  • Contacts
  • Photos or files

All typing data stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

Keyboard Input

The text typed using TorunHero Keyboard is processed locally on your device. We do not monitor, record, or store what you type.

Permissions

The app may request Internet permission only for updates and improvements. No personal data is collected.

Third-Party Services

The app may use Google Play Services which may collect limited information according to their privacy policy.

Children’s Privacy

TorunHero Keyboard does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time.
